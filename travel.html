Travel Guide

A dedicated planning card for every road trip — hotels, food, parking, and where to find the other parents.

Feb 14 · Away

Syracuse Weekend

Recommended hotel:

Marriott Syracuse Downtown — Club rate block "GWLAX26," 10 min from the JMA Wireless Dome.

Team hotel:

Not shareable this trip per team travel policy — families should book separately.

Friday dinner:

Dinosaur Bar-B-Que downtown, 6:30 PM — look for the reserved buff & blue table.

Saturday coffee meetup:

Cafe Kubal on Marshall St., 10:00 AM before the 1:00 PM draw.

Stadium parking:

Irving Garage, $10 flat rate, 5-minute walk to the Dome.

Postgame gathering:

Ale House Syracuse, right after final whistle — win or lose.

Nearest airport:

Syracuse Hancock International (SYR), 15 min from downtown.

Sightseeing if staying longer:

Green Lakes State Park and the Erie Canal Museum are both worth a Sunday morning.

Mar 07 · Away

Villanova Weekend

Recommended hotel:

Hampton Inn Philadelphia/King of Prussia — 10 min from campus, family rooms available.

Team hotel:

Shareable — Courtyard Villanova, ask the Travel Coordinator for the group code.

Friday dinner:

Maggiano's King of Prussia, 6:00 PM group reservation under "GW Parents."

Saturday coffee meetup:

Saxbys on Lancaster Ave, 10:30 AM ahead of the noon draw.

Stadium parking:

Free lot parking adjacent to Higgins Field, arrive early — it fills up.

Postgame gathering:

Kildare's Irish Pub on Lancaster Ave.

Nearest airport:

Philadelphia International (PHL), 30 min away.

Sightseeing if staying longer:

Valley Forge National Historical Park is 15 minutes from the hotel.

Mar 21 · Away

Davidson Weekend

Recommended hotel:

Hampton Inn Davidson — walkable to campus, family rooms available.

Team hotel:

TBD — check with Travel Coordinator closer to the trip.

Friday dinner:

Kindred Restaurant in downtown Davidson, 6:30 PM.

Saturday coffee meetup:

Summit Coffee on Main St., 9:30 AM before the noon draw.

Stadium parking:

Free lot at Wilson Field, short walk from the visitor lot.

Postgame gathering:

Toast Cafe, casual and close to the field.

Nearest airport:

Charlotte Douglas International (CLT), 25 min from Davidson.

Sightseeing if staying longer:

Lake Norman is 10 minutes away for a Sunday morning walk.

Each Travel Guide card is a template — the Travel Coordinator updates hotel blocks, meetup times, and postgame spots the week of each trip. See Contact to reach the Travel Coordinator directly.
