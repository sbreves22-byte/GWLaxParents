This month's spotlight

Meet the Reynolds Family

Parents of Ava Reynolds '28, from Baltimore, MD. Mark and Dana have made every home game since Ava's freshman fall-ball scrimmage, and they run the Club's carpool spreadsheet that's saved half the parent group from I-95 traffic. Ask them about the best coffee stop on the way to Foggy Bottom.

Last month's spotlight

Meet the Alvarez Family

Parents of Sofia Alvarez '26, from Richmond, VA. Sofia's senior season has the whole family making every trip — home or away — and they've become the unofficial welcome committee for new goalie families. They're always "happy to host" the night before a home game.
