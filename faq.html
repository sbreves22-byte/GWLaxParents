New to the club?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything a new parent asks in their first season.

Where do parents sit?

Most parents stand along the fence behind the home bench. There are bleachers on the other side of the field that some parents will sit in.

Are tickets required?

No — regular-season home games are free and open to the public.

Can families attend team meals?

No, but families are invited to the post game meals!

How do tailgates work?

The tailgate will start about two hours before the game. The athletes do not attend the tailgate. The tailgate will be on top of the hill; look for the buff and blue canopies. Parents are strongly encouraged to help set up and breakdown.

What happens on Senior Day?

Seniors and their families are introduced on the field before the game, each senior receives flowers sponsored by the Club, and the team banquet follows that evening.

How do I join the Parent Directory?

Email the Parent Coordinator with your player's name for verification, then set your own visibility preferences — see the Directory page for details.
