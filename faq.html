New to the club?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything a new parent asks in their first season.

Where do parents sit?

Home-side bleachers behind the team bench, marked with buff ribbon for family seating. Get there 20–30 minutes early for the best spots, especially for Parents Weekend.

Are tickets required?

No — regular-season home games are free and open to the public. NCAA Tournament games may require tickets; see the question below.

Can families attend team meals?

Most team meals are player- and coach-only, but the Club hosts a parent-inclusive pasta dinner before select home games — watch the Events Calendar for dates.

What's the NCAA tournament ticket process?

If the Revolutionaries qualify, the athletics department releases a family ticket link within 48 hours of Selection Sunday. The Club sends this out immediately over the text/email alert list.

What are the best hotels for home games?

For Parents Weekend and other big home dates, the Club blocks rooms at a hotel near campus — check the top of the Schedule page or ask the Travel Coordinator for the current code.

How do tailgates work?

Tailgates start two hours before first draw in Lot C. Look for the buff & blue canopy — bring a dish to share, and sign up for a slot on the Volunteer page if you can.

What happens on Senior Day?

Seniors and their families are introduced on the field before the game, each senior receives flowers sponsored by the Club, and the team banquet follows that evening.

How do I join the Parent Directory?

Email the Freshman Family Liaison with your player's name for verification, then set your own visibility preferences — see the Directory page for details.
