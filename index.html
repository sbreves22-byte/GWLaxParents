Who we are

Welcome to the GW Women's Lacrosse Parents Club

We're the volunteer network of parents and families behind every Revolutionaries home game, road trip, and season milestone. Founded by families who wanted to turn "strangers in the stands" into a real community, the Club exists for one reason: to make sure every player — and every parent — feels supported from the first draw of fall ball to the last whistle of the NCAA tournament.

Support the Players

Post game meals, senior gifts, and quiet behind-the-scenes help so the team can focus on their season.

Connect the Families

The Club GroupMe and our events help parents connect and feel like they are not alone.

Build Team Culture

Tailgates, banquets, senior day, and traditions that make Revolutionaries lacrosse feel like family, not just a schedule.
