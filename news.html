May 30, 2026

2026–2027 Season Preview

The 2027 season represents an important step forward for George Washington as head coach Colleen McCaffrey continues to build the Revolutionaries into a program capable of climbing the Atlantic 10 standings. After relying on a blend of experienced veterans and talented underclassmen in 2026, GW enters 2027 with a roster that should feature a strong sophomore and junior core that has already gained valuable Division I experience.

Replacing the leadership of the 2026 senior class won't be easy. Veterans who anchored the offense and defense leave behind significant production and experience, meaning the next generation will have to grow up quickly. Fortunately for the Revolutionaries, many of the rising seniors were starters, and the players expected to return were contributors before they became upperclassmen.
