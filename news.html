Away-Game Bus Sign-Up Now Open

Parents traveling to Syracuse for the season opener can reserve a seat on the family bus through the Volunteer page — seats are limited, so sign up early. The bus departs from the Mount Vernon Campus at 7:00 AM.

New Freshman Parent Welcome Guide Is Live

If you're new to the Club this year, start with the Welcome Guide on the Incoming Families page — move-in tips, local hotels, restaurants, and travel info for your first season.

The Revolutionaries return a strong senior class, anchored by attackers Grace Curry and Emma Curry. Coach McCaffrey's full preview will be shared at the preseason parent meeting in August.
