Home games

Home Game Day Details

Stadium

Mount Vernon Campus Turf

2100 Foxhall Road NW, Washington, DC 20007

Gates open one hour before first draw. Enter through the main athletics gate off Foxhall Road.

Parking

Where to Park

Visitor Lot C is closest to the field and opens 2 hours before kickoff. Overflow parking available in the West Deck — free on weekends.

View Parking Map
Tailgate

Parent Tailgate Spot

Look for the buff & blue canopy at the far end of Lot C. Setup begins 2 hours before first draw — bring a dish to share!

Tickets

Getting In

Home games are free and open to the public; no ticket required. Reserved family seating is marked with buff ribbon on the home side.

Athletics Ticket Page
Weather notes

What to Expect

The turf sits close to the Potomac — bring a layer even on sunny days, and check the forecast the morning of. Games are rarely postponed for rain; lightning delays are announced via the Club's text/email alert list.
